A story that makes you believe in love ❤️… more than astrology
Nalla Neram
Two strangers meet in an arranged marriage setting — he trusts feelings, she trusts fate written in the stars. Conversations become comfort, and comfort slowly becomes love. But the horoscope says they are not meant for each other. When the heart finds its person, should destiny be decided by planets… or by love?

Directed by Selvaraj Ganesan
